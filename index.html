About Me

I am a BA graduate from the University of the Arts London, London College of Communication. I attended the Makers Academy for 12 weeks where I completed their 12 week intensive coding course. Following this I took on a 6 month internship at a graphic design agency, 'Acuity', in Watford which I have just finished.

I enjoy being active by going to the gym and playing ultimate frisbee. I particularly like taking part in team based sports and being social. In my spare time I also enjoy films & video games.

I have been mainly based in Buckinghamshire & Hertfordshire, other than spending three years living and studying in London.

I am looking for work in Graphic Design & Front End Web Development. I am primarily looking for a full time role but happy to take on freelance work as well.
