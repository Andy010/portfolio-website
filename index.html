About Me

I am a BA graduate from the University of the Arts london. I have attended the Makers Academy learn to code in 12 weeks course. More recently I have just finished an internship at a graphic design agency in Watford called Acuity.

I enjoy being active by going to the gym and playing ultimate frisbee. I prefer to play team sports and admit I enjoy video games.

I have lived in the Buckinghamshire area all my life except for living in London whilst at university.
